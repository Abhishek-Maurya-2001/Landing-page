About Us

Babu Banarasi Das Institute of Technology & Management (BBDITM) was established in the year 1998 in Lucknow. The college is a private institution approved by AICTE and affiliated with Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow. It is recognized by the NBA. The institute has signed about 10 MoUs with several companies such as Oracle, Amazon, Infosys, and many more.

BBDITM offers courses such as B. Tech, M. Tech courses, and an MBA program. Electronics and Communication Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Information Technology are some of the B. Tech courses offered by BBDITM. Students are admitted based on the scores of the entrance exams such as JEE MAIN, CUET, CAT, and GATE which is then followed by UPTAC counseling.
